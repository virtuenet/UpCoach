# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account & Subscription

### How do I create an account?
Download the UpCoach app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Open the app and tap "Sign Up". You can register using your email address or sign in with Google/Apple.

### How do I reset my password?
1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### What subscription plans are available?
UpCoach offers three plans:
- **Free**: 5 habits, 3 goals, limited AI coaching
- **Premium ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited habits & goals, full AI access, all integrations
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for teams

### How do I upgrade to Premium?
1. Go to Profile → Settings → Subscription
2. Select "Upgrade to Premium"
3. Choose monthly or annual billing
4. Complete payment through App Store/Play Store

### How do I cancel my subscription?
- **iOS**: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → UpCoach → Cancel
- **Android**: Play Store → Subscriptions → UpCoach → Cancel
- Subscription remains active until the end of the billing period

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google depending on your platform. Contact their support for refund requests within 14 days of purchase.

## Habits

### How many habits can I track?
- Free plan: Up to 5 active habits
- Premium/Enterprise: Unlimited habits

### What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak will reset to 0, but don't worry! Your overall completion data is preserved. Focus on starting a new streak today.

### Can I pause a habit?
Yes! Swipe left on a habit and tap "Pause". Paused habits won't count against your daily goals or affect your streak.

### How do I delete a habit?
Swipe left on the habit → Tap "Delete" → Confirm. Note: This permanently removes all completion history for this habit.

### Can I reorder my habits?
Yes, tap and hold a habit, then drag it to your preferred position.

### What's the difference between skip and incomplete?
- **Skip**: Intentionally not doing the habit (vacation, sick day). Doesn't break streak.
- **Incomplete**: Simply not logging completion. Breaks streak at day's end.

## Goals

### How do I create milestones?
1. Open a goal
2. Tap "Add Milestone"
3. Enter milestone details and target date
4. Milestones help track incremental progress

### Can I link habits to goals?
Yes! When creating or editing a habit, you can associate it with a specific goal. This helps track how your daily habits contribute to larger objectives.

### What happens when I complete a goal?
The goal moves to "Completed" status. You'll receive a celebration notification and can view it in your achievements history.

### Can I edit a goal after creation?
Yes, tap on the goal to open details, then tap the edit icon to modify any field.

## Wellness & Integrations

### Which devices are supported?
- Oura Ring (Gen 2 & 3)
- Fitbit (all models)
- Apple Watch (via Apple Health)
- Google Fit compatible devices
- Garmin (coming soon)
- Whoop (coming soon)

### How often does wellness data sync?
Data syncs automatically every hour when you're connected to the internet. You can also manually sync by pulling down on the Wellness screen.

### Why isn't my device syncing?
1. Check that the device is connected to the internet
2. Verify authorization in Settings → Integrations
3. Try disconnecting and reconnecting the device
4. Ensure the device's own app is syncing properly

### Is my health data secure?
Absolutely. All wellness data is:
- Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Never sold to third parties
- Deletable at any time

### How do I disconnect a device?
Settings → Integrations → Select device → "Disconnect"

## Notifications & Reminders

### How do I set up reminders?
1. Edit a habit
2. Enable "Reminder"
3. Set your preferred time(s)
4. Choose which days to be reminded

### I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?
1. Check that notifications are enabled in your phone's settings
2. Verify UpCoach has notification permissions
3. Ensure "Do Not Disturb" isn't active
4. Check that battery optimization isn't blocking notifications (Android)

### Can I customize notification sounds?
iOS allows custom sounds through system settings. Android users can change sounds in Settings → Notifications.

### How do I turn off all notifications?
Settings → Notifications → Toggle off "Push Notifications"

## Troubleshooting

### The app is running slowly
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Check for app updates
3. Clear app cache (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache)
4. Restart your device

### I lost my streak but completed the habit
If you completed a habit but forgot to log it:
1. Go to the habit
2. Tap on the calendar view
3. Select the missed date
4. Mark as complete
Note: This only works within 24 hours of the missed day.

### Sync issues between devices
1. Ensure you're logged into the same account
2. Check internet connection
3. Force sync: Pull down on main screen
4. Log out and log back in

### App crashes on launch
1. Update to the latest version
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall the app
4. Contact support if issue persists
