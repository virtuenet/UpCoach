## Creating Your First Habit

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Open the Habits Tab**
   - Tap the **Habits** icon in the bottom navigation

2. **Tap "Add Habit"**
   - Click the + button in the top right

3. **Choose a Category**
   - Health & Fitness
   - Mindfulness
   - Productivity
   - Learning
   - Social
   - Custom

4. **Define Your Habit**
   - **Name**: e.g., "Morning Exercise"
   - **Description**: Optional details
   - **Icon**: Choose a visual identifier

5. **Set Frequency**
   - **Daily**: Every day
   - **Weekly**: Specific days (e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri)
   - **Custom**: X times per week

6. **Configure Reminders**
   - Enable push notifications
   - Set reminder time(s)
   - Choose reminder days

### Habit Tracking Tips

- **Start Small**: Begin with 2-3 habits
- **Be Specific**: "Walk 10 minutes" vs "Exercise"
- **Stack Habits**: Link new habits to existing routines
- **Track Consistently**: Log completions daily

## Setting Goals

### Goal Types

| Type | Duration | Examples |
|------|----------|----------|
| Short-term | 1-4 weeks | Read 2 books |
| Medium-term | 1-3 months | Learn cooking basics |
| Long-term | 3-12 months | Run a marathon |

### Creating a Goal

1. **Navigate to Goals**
   - Tap the **Goals** tab

2. **Tap "New Goal"**
   - Click the + button

3. **Define Your Goal**
   - **Title**: Clear, actionable statement
   - **Description**: Why this matters
   - **Target Date**: Realistic deadline
   - **Type**: Short/Medium/Long-term

4. **Add Milestones**
   - Break down into smaller achievements
   - Set milestone dates
   - Track progress

### SMART Goals Framework

- **S**pecific: What exactly do you want to achieve?
- **M**easurable: How will you track progress?
- **A**chievable: Is it realistic?
- **R**elevant: Does it align with your values?
- **T**ime-bound: When will you complete it?

## Connecting Wellness Devices

### Supported Integrations

| Device/App | Data Synced |
|------------|-------------|
| Oura Ring | Sleep, Readiness, Activity |
| Fitbit | Steps, Sleep, Heart Rate |
| Apple Health | All health metrics |
| Google Fit | Steps, Activity, Sleep |

### Connection Steps

1. **Go to Settings**
   - Tap Profile → Settings → Integrations

2. **Select Device**
   - Choose your wellness device/app

3. **Authorize Access**
   - Log in to your device account
   - Grant UpCoach permission to read data

4. **Sync Data**
   - Initial sync may take a few minutes
   - Data updates automatically every hour

### Privacy & Data

- Your wellness data is encrypted
- You control what data is shared
- Data can be deleted at any time
- We never sell your data

## Tips for Success

### Building Consistency

1. **Start with Why**
   - Connect habits to deeper motivations
   - Write down your reasons

2. **Environment Design**
   - Remove friction for good habits
   - Add friction for bad habits

3. **Habit Stacking**
   - "After I [current habit], I will [new habit]"
   - Link to existing routines

4. **Track Progress**
   - Review your streaks weekly
   - Celebrate milestones

5. **Be Patient**
   - Habits take 21-66 days to form
   - Focus on consistency, not perfection
